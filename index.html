How Zirclo works

Whether you need a job done or you do the jobs — Zirclo is built to make the whole thing simple, safe and transparent.

For Homeowners
1
Create a free account
Sign up with your email and verify your mobile number. Takes 2 minutes. No payment details required.
2
Post your job
Describe what you need, your postcode, budget and timeline. You can also specify certification requirements like Gas Safe or NICEIC. Always free to post.
3
Receive quotes from verified trades
Local trades matching your job are notified and can send you a quote. You see their full profile — ratings, reviews, certifications, gallery and pricing — before making a decision.
4
Choose your trade and get it done
Accept the quote you're happy with. The trade is notified and you agree a time. Job done — leave a review so the next homeowner knows what to expect.
5
Save your favourites
Found a great trade? Save them to your account for next time. Set a reminder for annual services like boiler checks so you never forget.
For Trades
1
Create your profile
Build your Zirclo profile with your trade, skills, certifications, pricing and work gallery. First 10 sign-ups get free lifetime membership.
2
Get alerted to nearby jobs
When a homeowner posts a job matching your trade within your coverage area, you're notified instantly by SMS. Pro members get priority alerts.
3
Send a quote
Review the job details and send your quote through Zirclo. The homeowner can see your full profile alongside your quote — your reviews and ratings do the selling for you.
4
Complete the job and build your reputation
Every completed job adds to your trust score and review history. The more jobs you complete, the higher you appear in search results — and the more jobs you win.
Common Questions
Is it really free for homeowners?+
Yes, always. Posting a job, receiving quotes, browsing profiles and leaving reviews is completely free for homeowners. Zirclo makes money from trade subscriptions, not from homeowners.
How are tradespeople verified?+
Every trade verifies their mobile number and email on signup. Certification badges (Gas Safe, NICEIC, DBS etc.) are awarded once the trade uploads their official documentation, which is reviewed by the Zirclo team. Reviews from real homeowners are also publicly visible on every profile.
What if something goes wrong with the work?+
Zirclo facilitates the connection between homeowners and trades. Any disputes about the quality of work are between the homeowner and the trade directly. We strongly recommend ensuring any trade you hire has appropriate insurance for the work being carried out. Zirclo Home subscribers get additional dispute support.
Can I cancel my trade subscription?+
Yes. You can cancel at any time and your plan will remain active until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees.
Does Zirclo take a commission on jobs?+
No. Zirclo charges trades a flat monthly subscription — not a percentage of each job. What you earn is yours. No hidden fees, no commission.
