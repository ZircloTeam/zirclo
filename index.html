Zirclo is designed to make the whole thing simple, safe and transparent for both sides.

Common questions

Is it free to post a request?+
Yes. Posting a request is always free. A £1 refundable deposit is required to keep requests genuine — this is returned to you when your job is filled. Zirclo Home members post with no deposit at all.
How does Zirclo verify professionals?+
Every professional on Zirclo verifies their identity before their profile goes live. For tradespeople, we also check relevant certifications — Gas Safe registrations are verified against the official register, NICEIC membership is checked, and DBS certificates are reviewed. Professionals cannot display a badge they haven't earned.
What is Zirclo Pay?+
Zirclo Pay is an optional payment protection feature. When you choose it, your payment is held securely and only released to the professional once you confirm the work is complete. If a dispute arises, the funds stay frozen until it's resolved. There's a flat £3 fee per booking, paid by the customer. The professional pays nothing.
How much does it cost to join as a professional?+
The first 10 professionals to sign up get the Starter plan free for life. After that, plans start at £15/month. All plans include a 14-day free trial. Zirclo charges a flat monthly fee — zero commission on any booking, ever.
What is Local Help?+
Local Help is a separate tier for everyday tasks that don't require professional qualifications — lawn mowing, cleaning, car washing, flat pack assembly, and more. All Local Help members are ID verified. Local Help members cannot offer or carry out trade-level work. £5/month to list, zero commission on earnings.

Last updated: March 2026 · Zirclo Ltd, registered in England & Wales

These Terms and Conditions govern your use of the Zirclo platform. By creating an account or posting a request, you agree to these terms in full. They are written in plain English and are designed to protect everyone who uses Zirclo.

1. What Zirclo Is

Zirclo is an online marketplace that connects customers who need services with self-employed professionals who can provide them. We are not an employer, recruitment agency, or contractor. Any contract for work is formed between the customer and the professional directly. Zirclo is not party to that contract.

2. Accounts & Verification

All users must provide accurate information when creating an account. Mobile number verification is required for all accounts. Professionals must provide accurate qualification details — uploading false or expired credentials is grounds for immediate permanent account removal. You are responsible for keeping your login details secure.

3. Callout Fees

Callout fees are set by the professional and paid directly to the professional. Zirclo takes no portion of any callout fee. Callout fees are non-refundable if the customer cancels with less than 2 hours notice. If a professional fails to attend without notice, they must refund the callout fee directly to the customer.

4. Quotes & Price Lock

Once a quote is accepted by the customer in the app, the price is locked. The professional may not charge more than 15% above the agreed quote without submitting a new written quote through the app and having it accepted by the customer before any additional work begins.

5. Zirclo Pay

Zirclo Pay is optional. When used, the customer pays a £3 flat fee and their payment is held securely until they confirm the work is complete. Payment is released to the professional only after confirmation. If a dispute is raised, funds are frozen until resolved. Zirclo's decision in disputes is final.

6. Reviews

Reviews are blind — neither party sees the other's review until both have submitted, or 7 days have passed. Only customers who have booked and paid for a service through Zirclo can leave a review. Offering incentives for reviews is prohibited and may result in account termination.

7. Disputes

Disputes must be raised within 7 days of job completion. Both parties have 48 hours to submit their side. Clear-cut cases are resolved automatically. Zirclo will review and decide on ambiguous or high-value disputes (over £150) within 5 working days. Zirclo's decision is final.

8. No-Shows

A professional who fails to attend a confirmed booking without giving at least 2 hours notice will receive a no-show strike. Three no-show strikes result in automatic account suspension pending review. Professionals with active strikes have reduced search placement.

9. Subscriptions & Refunds

Professional subscriptions are billed monthly in advance. You may cancel at any time and your subscription will remain active until the end of the billing period. No partial refunds are issued for unused time. The 14-day free trial applies to new accounts only.

10. Prohibited Conduct

The following are prohibited on Zirclo: impersonating another person or professional; posting false qualifications or credentials; using the platform to arrange work and then completing it off-platform to avoid subscription fees (applicable to all account types including Local Help); harassment of any user; sharing another user's personal data; and attempting to manipulate search rankings or reviews. Breaches may result in immediate account termination.

11. Liability

Zirclo is an introducer only. We are not liable for the quality of work carried out by professionals, for any property damage, personal injury, or loss arising from a booking. Our maximum liability to any user is the lesser of £100 or the total subscription fees paid in the preceding 12 months.

12. Zirclo Local Help — Fair Use

Local Help membership permits listing and responding to Local Help category requests only. It expressly prohibits: offering or carrying out trade-level work through a Local Help booking; sharing personal contact details or business information in a profile or messages before a job is confirmed; holding simultaneous Local Help and professional accounts; taking clients met through Zirclo to work with off-platform; and review manipulation. Breaches result in account termination without refund. Three unresolved reports against a Local Help account trigger automatic suspension.

13. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of England and Wales. Any dispute that cannot be resolved through the platform dispute process shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales.

This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and your rights under UK GDPR. Questions: [email protected]

What data we collect

Account data: Name, email, mobile number, postcode.

Profile data: For professionals — trade type, qualifications, pricing, bio, gallery photos, coverage area. For customers — job history, saved professionals, preferences.

Activity data: Requests posted, quotes sent and received, messages, reviews, disputes, and payment records.

Device data: IP address, device type, browser, pages visited. Collected automatically.

Payment data: Handled by Stripe. We do not store full card details — only transaction amounts, dates, and outcomes.

How we use your data

To operate the platform and match customers with professionals. To verify identities and qualifications. To send booking notifications and updates. To process payments via Stripe. To investigate disputes and enforce our terms. To improve the platform using anonymised usage data.

Who we share data with

Twilio — SMS verification and notifications.

We do not sell personal data to third parties. We do not share individually identifiable data with advertisers. We may share anonymised, aggregated data for research or business purposes.
